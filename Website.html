Description

Crypto trading bots are designed to trade cryptocurrency assets on the user’s behalf. Without the bot, the user would typically have to sit in front of their device and pick which cryptocurrency to buy and sell. The problem with that is that there is a high risk of losing as it is a gamble to just pick a coin and buy.

The bot would gather market data, interpret it, calculate the potential risk, and execute the action (buy or sell). The crypto trading bot follows one rule, buy low and sell high, meaning when a price of a coin goes down, the bot would buy the coin using a small percentage of the money in your balance, and would continue to buy if the price keeps going down. Same goes when the price goes up, the bot would start selling a small amount to make sure you have profits and continue to sell if the price keeps going up.

A key component in crypto trading bots have is market data analysis, this module of the bot will save raw market data from different sources which will help it decide whether to buy or sell a specific cryptocurrency. It also uses the market data to calculate the potential risk in the market. Based on the information, the bot will decide how much to invest or trade. If the bot interprets the data that the trade has a high risk for potential downside, it will only invest a little. If the bot interprets the data as low risk and high potential for big profit, the bot will invest more. Sometimes it’s not a good idea to buy a token in bulk but in some situations call for immediate purchases. The bot will help the user decide that.

By using a trading cryptocurrency bot, this can eliminate delays and human error, if a bot is receiving the correct data and has suitable algorithms, it can trade crypto with a higher chance of profit.

If you have done crypto trading before you will know that your emotions will be all over the place, by using the bot, the user will not have to worry about having stress as the trading bot takes every single decision based on data. Although experienced traders may overpower their emotions and make rational decisions, but that may not be the case with beginners.

Although there are a lot of benefits to having a crypto trading bot, there are also downside. First one of the downsides is that this is cryptocurrency, and it is a form of gambling and can also have extreme unpredictable outcome. Situations like this can happen, for example, the start of the corona virus pandemic, impacted the crypto market heavily and caused it to plummet. Situations like this, it is better to trust your instincts than using a trading bot.

The benefits of having a trading bot can only be achieved if the programming is done correctly, having programming errors can impact the efficiency of the bot.

Tools and Technologies

To make a crypto trading bot, a computer is required. There are many programs that can be used to make a trading bot. A person can make it from scratch or use a platform that allows a person to customise the bot. A few examples are, Hummingbot, Trality. Hummingbot is an open-source command-line interface that lets the user build their own automated trading bot. Similarly to Hummingbot, Trality provides a graphical UI for a user to build their bot.
